Parking Ticket Machine Project

In this project during my internship at the Punta Cana International Airport, I designed a simple piece that would go on to save the technicians at the maintaince department plenty of valuable time. The problem with the ticket machines was that when it started to rain, the ticket machines would take in a lot of water through the ticket insertion slot and cause failures, which required a maintinance technician to fix. My simple and elegant solution solved the problem by placing a small hood over the slot machine which was attached using epoxy ensuring that gap between the slot and the hood was water proof. Before the end of my internship, I was able to take my project up to the director of land side operations at the airport, and implement my part on to every parking ticket machine in the airport.
